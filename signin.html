Our Privacy Policy

Overview

This policy describes what information we collect and how it is used and shared. Our goal is to help churches by providing administrative and communication tools that are essential to achieve their mission. Remember once you have signed in you can fully manage what information you would like us to send you.

What do we collect?

Information you provide.

We collect the content and other information you provide when you use our services, including when you register with your church and when you interact with the various features that the church has enabled on the application.

Your interaction with our services

We keep track of the groups you lead or of which you are a member. As a church we must give you permission to use the groups interface. You can request this access from the application. We also keep track of any lists you are a part of or events you have registered for. At any time you can remove yourself from these lists and unregister for events.

Device Information

We will at times use the following information from your device:

  • Location data is used to set the default location for some of the modules that allow you to interact with a map. E.g. when you indicate the location of your home address we will use your current location to default the map interface.
  • We will access the OS data of your device in order to provide you with the best user experience when you are using the application. We do not store this information on our servers.

How do we use data?

Communications

We use the data collected above to communicate with you.

  • Your news feed can be fully customized so that you only get the news you want to get. You can manage this content in the subscriptions section of the application.
  • You will also at times receive communication from us if you are a member of certain groups.
  • We use the events tool to communicate with you if you have registered for an event.
  • At times we will also send out communications to all members of certain lists in the church. You can at any time remove yourself from these lists using the application.
Manage the church

Some of the information e.g. group information we use to help manage and pastor the church.

Sharing your data

Under no circumstances is your data ever shared with third parties.

Communication

You can manage what news and information is sent to you from the church. we communicate with you in the following ways.

  • Email
  • Push Notifications
  • SMS/Text Messaging

Managing your content

You can manage your profile with the church from inside the application. Using the app you can fully customize what features you would like to be linked to your profile. You can also control what communications are sent to you using the news subscription modules.
